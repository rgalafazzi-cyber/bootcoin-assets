# Bootcoin

Bootcoin is a utility token designed to power access, rewards, and exclusivity in the sneaker ecosystem.

## Vision
To create a community of collectors and enthusiasts who value rarity, identity, and access.

## Utility
- Earn BOOT through purchases
- Unlock exclusive sneaker drops
- Access private sales
- Redeem BOOT for discounts

## Tokenomics
- Total Supply: 1,000,000 BOOT
- Distribution:
  - 30% Rewards
  - 25% Treasury
  - 20% Liquidity
  - 15% Founders
  - 10% Marketing

## Philosophy
Bootcoin is not just a currency — it is a key to a curated ecosystem.

---

Bootcoin — for the few, not the many.
